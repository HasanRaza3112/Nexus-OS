# Nexus OS — Vision

## What This Is
Nexus OS is a business operating system, being built to support a solo freelance Unity developer's transition into a sustainable consulting business. It replaces disconnected tools (spreadsheets, scattered docs) with one integrated system, scoped to actual need rather than built speculatively ahead of revenue.

## Current Phase
Phase 0 (Business Discovery) complete. Phase 1 (Requirements) starting for the minimal viable scope below.

## Launch Service (what the business sells right now)
- Unity Bug Fixing
- Unity Feature / Mechanic Development
- Performance Optimization

These were selected because they are the only services with real, demonstrable proof of work (a finished Unity project) as of Phase 0. All other services from the original master catalog (XR/VR, AI integration, SDK development, CTO-as-a-service, etc.) are deferred to later phases and are NOT in scope for the current build.

## Minimal Viable Nexus OS (current build scope)
Scoped tightly to what the launch service actually needs to operate:
1. Lead/inquiry tracking (lightweight CRM)
2. Quote/proposal generator for fixed-scope Unity jobs
3. Basic invoice tracking once a client pays

No other module (Knowledge Base, Learning Hub, Content Studio, Analytics, etc.) is in scope until there is real usage data to justify it.

## Long-Term Vision (unchanged, deferred)
The original full-scope vision — a complete business OS covering CRM, Project Management, Portfolio CMS, Proposal Builder, Invoicing, Knowledge Base, Learning Hub, AI Workspace, Content Studio, Job Tracker, Finance Dashboard, Analytics, BI — remains the long-term direction. It is sequenced, not abandoned.
